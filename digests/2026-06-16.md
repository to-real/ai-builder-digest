## Training Data — LIVE: Jensen Huang on Building the Dynamo of the Intelligence Age
*Training Data*

NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang reframes the AI revolution as a shift from a retrieval-based computer industry to a generative one, where "AI factories" act as the new dynamos converting electrons into intelligence (tokens). He predicts a future internet dominated by billions of agentic systems communicating with each other and outlines a five-layer investment cake: energy, computing, infrastructure, models, and applications. Crucially, Huang dismisses fears of job replacement, arguing that while AI automates tasks, it elevates professions by shifting focus from execution to purpose—radiologists didn't disappear when computer vision arrived; they became more productive and in demand.

NVIDIA CEO 黄仁勋将 AI 革命重新定义为从基于检索的计算机行业向生成式行业的转变，其中“AI 工厂”充当新的发电机，将电子转化为智能。他预测未来的互联网将由数十亿个相互通信的 agent 系统主导，并概述了一个五层投资蛋糕：能源、计算、基础设施、模型和应用。黄仁勋驳斥了对工作被取代的担忧，他认为虽然 AI 自动化了任务，但通过将重心从执行转移到目的，它提升了职业水平——例如，当计算机视觉出现时，放射科医生并没有消失，反而变得更高效且需求更大。
