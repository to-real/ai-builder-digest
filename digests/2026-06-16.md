## Training Data — LIVE: Jensen Huang on Building the Dynamo of the Intelligence Age

*Training Data*

**The intelligence revolution is shifting computing from retrieval to generation, where "AI factories" produce intelligence as a commodity, and the only existential threat is refusing to use these tools.**

NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang frames the current era as the transition from retrieval-based computing, where data centers simply store files, to generative computing, where "AI factories" produce intelligence in real-time. He describes NVIDIA's systems as modern-day dynamos that convert electrons into tokens—numbers that represent language, biology, physics, and robotics. Huang predicts a future where the internet serves not just 1 billion humans, but 100 billion agents communicating continuously, creating a massive new industrial layer. He outlines a five-layer investment stack: energy, chips, infrastructure, models, and applications. Dismissing fears of AI replacing humans, he argues that AI elevates the purpose of work by automating tasks while increasing demand for human judgment, citing the growth of radiology jobs despite the prevalence of computer vision. "You may or may not lose a job to an AI. But you will absolutely lose a job to someone who uses AI."

NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang 将当前时代定义为从基于检索的计算（数据中心仅存储文件）向生成式计算的转变，在后者中，“AI 工厂”实时生成智能。他将 NVIDIA 的系统描述为现代发电机，将电子转化为 token——代表语言、生物学、物理学和机器人的数字。Huang 预测，未来的互联网将不仅服务于 10 亿人类，还将服务于 1000 亿个持续通信的 agent，从而创造一个巨大的新工业层。他概述了一个五层投资堆栈：能源、芯片、基础设施、模型和应用。他驳斥了 AI 取代人类的恐惧，认为 AI 通过自动化任务提升了工作的目的性，并增加了对人类判断的需求，并以放射科医生工作岗位在计算机视觉普及后的增长为例证。“你可能会输给 AI，也可能不会。但你绝对会输给那些使用 AI 的人。”
