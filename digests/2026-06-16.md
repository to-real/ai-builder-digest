## Training Data — LIVE: Jensen Huang on Building the Dynamo of the Intelligence Age
*Training Data*

The Takeaway: The shift from retrieval-based to generative computing represents a new industrial revolution where intelligence is manufactured like electricity, and the winners will be those who use AI to elevate their purpose rather than just automate tasks.

Jensen Huang, CEO of NVIDIA, frames the current AI era as the "Intelligence Age," comparing the production of AI tokens to the generation of electricity by dynamos three centuries ago. He argues that while the last 60 years of computing were about retrieving stored data, the future is about generating intelligence in real-time for every interaction.

Huang presents a "five-layer cake" investment thesis: energy, computing infrastructure, data centers, AI models, and applications. He emphasizes that the AI industry is currently a $1 trillion investment within a potential $20 trillion annual ecosystem. Crucially, he challenges the fear of job loss, distinguishing between "tasks" (typing, coding) and "purpose" (problem-solving, diagnosing). Using radiology as an example, he notes that despite computer vision automating the analysis of scans, the demand for radiologists increased because their true purpose—diagnosing disease with doctors—became more productive and accessible. He asserts, "You may or may not lose a job to an AI. But you will absolutely lose a job to someone who uses AI."

核心观点：从基于检索的计算向生成式计算的转变代表了一场新的工业革命，其中智能像电力一样被制造，而赢家将是那些利用 AI 提升目标而非仅仅自动化任务的人。

NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang 将当前的 AI 时代定义为“智能时代”，将 AI token 的生产比作三个世纪前发电机产生的电力。他认为，过去 60 年的计算主要是关于检索存储的数据，而未来则是为每一次交互实时生成智能。

Huang 提出了一个“五层蛋糕”投资论点：能源、计算基础设施、数据中心、AI 模型和应用程序。他强调，AI 产业目前是潜在 20 万亿美元年度生态系统中的 1 万亿美元投资。至关重要的是，他挑战了对失业的恐惧，区分了“任务”（打字、编码）和“目标”（解决问题、诊断）。他以放射科为例，指出尽管计算机视觉自动化了扫描分析，但对放射科医生的需求却增加了，因为他们真正的目标——与医生一起诊断疾病——变得更加高效和可及。他断言：“你可能会也可能不会因为 AI 而失去工作，但你绝对会因为使用 AI 的人而失去工作。”
