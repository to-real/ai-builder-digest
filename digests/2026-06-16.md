## LIVE: Jensen Huang on Building the Dynamo of the Intelligence Age
*Training Data*

The Takeaway: The intelligence revolution represents a shift from retrieval-based computing to generative intelligence factories, creating a multi-trillion dollar industrial stack that will elevate human purpose rather than replace it.

Jensen Huang, CEO of NVIDIA, frames the current era as the "intelligence revolution," comparable historically to the advent of electricity and the internet. He explains that computing is fundamentally changing from a retrieval-based system—where we access stored files—to a generative one where intelligence is produced in real-time. NVIDIA's role, he describes, is building the "AI factory" or the new "dynamo" of the age, machines that convert energy and electrons into tokens (intelligence) that will eventually cocoon the planet. Huang outlines the AI industry as a five-layer cake comprising energy, computing hardware, infrastructure, models, and applications, representing a massive investment opportunity. Contrary to fears of job displacement, he argues that AI will elevate jobs by automating tasks while leaving the higher-level purpose to humans, citing the radiology field as an example where demand for experts increased alongside automation. "You may or may not lose a job to an AI. But you will absolutely lose a job to someone who uses AI," he warns, urging engagement over fear.

核心观点：智能革命代表了从基于检索的计算向生成式智能工厂的转变，这将创造一个数万亿美元的工业堆栈，并将提升人类的目标而非取代它。

NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang 将当前时代定义为“智能革命”，在历史上可与电力和互联网的出现相提并论。他解释说，计算正在从根本上从基于检索的系统（我们访问存储文件的地方）转变为生成式系统，即智能实时产生的系统。他描述 NVIDIA 的角色是构建“AI 工厂”或新时代的新“发电机”，这些机器将能量和电子转化为 token（智能），最终将覆盖整个地球。Huang 将 AI 产业概述为一个包含能源、计算硬件、基础设施、模型和应用层的五层蛋糕，代表了巨大的投资机会。与对工作岗位被取代的担忧相反，他认为 AI 将通过自动化任务来提升工作，同时将更高层次的目标留给人类，他以放射学领域为例，指出随着自动化的引入，对专家的需求反而增加了。“你可能会也可能不会输给 AI 而失去工作，但你绝对会输给那个使用 AI 的人，”他警告道，敦促人们参与而不是恐惧。
