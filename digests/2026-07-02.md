## Why Hardware-Software Co-Design Is AI's Real 100x: Dylan Patel of SemiAnalysis

*Training Data*

**The biggest AI efficiency gains come from co-optimizing hardware, software, and models simultaneously, not from improving one layer in isolation.**

最大的 AI 效率提升来自于同时协同优化硬件、软件和模型，而不是单独改进某一层。

Dylan Patel, founder of SemiAnalysis, argues that the industry obsession with individual layer improvements—like new GPU architectures or better kernels—misses the forest for the trees. The real 100x multiplier comes from hardware-software co-design, where models like DeepSeek are architected specifically for chips like Hopper or Blackwell. He highlights InferenceX, a benchmarking initiative that tracks the Pareto optimal curve of throughput versus latency daily, revealing that "one size fits all" infrastructure is dying. Patel also predicts that while space data centers won't matter in the next 3-5 years, they will dominate in 20 years due to terrestrial power constraints. He notes that NVIDIA backs Neo Clouds to create a multipolar world, preventing hyperscalers like Google and Amazon from exerting too much control over the compute supply chain.

SemiAnalysis 创始人 Dylan Patel 认为，行业对单层改进（如新的 GPU 架构或更好的内核）的痴迷是见树不见林。真正的 100 倍乘数效应来自于软硬件协同设计，即像 DeepSeek 这样的模型是专门为 Hopper 或 Blackwell 等芯片架构设计的。他重点介绍了 InferenceX，这是一个每天跟踪吞吐量与延迟帕累托最优曲线的基准测试倡议，揭示了“一刀切”的基础设施正在消亡。Patel 还预测，虽然太空数据中心在未来 3-5 年内不会成为主流，但由于陆基电力限制，它们将在 20 年后占据主导地位。他指出，NVIDIA 支持 Neo Clouds 是为了创造一个多极世界，防止 Google 和 Amazon 等超大规模厂商对计算供应链施加过多控制。
