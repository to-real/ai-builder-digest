## No Priors — How Nuclear Will Unlock Energy Abundance with Valar Atomics Founder Isaiah Taylor

*No Priors*

**Nuclear energy's bottleneck is hardware iteration, not design; Valar Atomics is proving this by manufacturing reactors and verticalizing the supply chain to achieve energy abundance.**

Isaiah Taylor, Founder and CEO of Valar Atomics, argues that the nuclear industry has been stuck in a "modeling and simulation" loop for decades, failing to build physical hardware. Valar is taking a different approach by treating nuclear as a hardware execution problem, focusing on simplicity and speed. They recently turned on the first advanced reactor to make power by a startup, demonstrating a "tick rate" (iteration speed) that far exceeds traditional players. Taylor emphasizes that nuclear needs its "Ford moment"—shifting from civil infrastructure construction to manufacturing. By verticalizing everything from concrete mixing to reactor protection systems, Valar has cut costs and timelines drastically. He believes that making energy 10x cheaper through nuclear will unlock "hyper techno industrialism," where the cost of physical goods approaches the cost of the energy required to make them.

核能的瓶颈是硬件迭代，而非设计；Valar Atomics 正通过制造反应堆和垂直整合供应链来证明这一点，以实现能源丰富。Valar Atomics 的创始人兼 CEO Isaiah Taylor 认为，核工业几十年来一直停留在“建模与模拟”的循环中，未能建造实体硬件。Valar 采取了一种不同的方法，将核能视为一个硬件执行问题，专注于简单性和速度。他们最近启动了第一家由初创公司发电的先进反应堆，展示了远超传统参与者的“tick rate”（迭代速度）。Taylor 强调，核能需要它的“福特时刻”——从土木基础设施建设转向制造业。通过垂直整合从混凝土搅拌到反应堆保护系统的所有环节，Valar 大幅削减了成本和时间表。他相信，通过核能将能源成本降低 10 倍，将解锁“超技术工业主义”，届时实体商品的成本将趋近于制造它们所需的能源成本。
