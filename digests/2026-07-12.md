## Stripe's AI Chief: How AI Agents Will Buy, Sell, and Pay

*The MAD Podcast with Matt Turck*

**The next evolution of agentic commerce involves agents running entire businesses as "micro firms," not just executing transactions.**

Emily Sense, Head of Data and AI at Stripe, explains that while we currently see agents mostly assisting with discovery and checkout (Level 2 autonomy), the future lies in agents that autonomously buy, sell, provision infrastructure, and generate profits. This shift requires new infrastructure like "vibe deployment" (Stripe Projects) to remove the friction of integrating services, and "streaming payments" via stablecoins to handle real-time, machine-speed microtransactions. Sense also highlights the rise of solopreneurs—5 million in the US alone earning over $100k—enabled by AI handling both coding and operations. However, she warns of "token theft," a new form of fraud where bad actors steal API tokens to resell services or generate content, affecting over one in six signups at AI companies.

代理商务的下一个演进阶段涉及 Agent 作为“微型公司”运营整个业务，而不仅仅是执行交易。Stripe 数据与 AI 负责人 Emily Sense 解释说，虽然目前我们主要看到 Agent 在发现产品和结账方面提供帮助（2 级自主性），但未来的关键在于那些能够自主购买、销售、配置基础设施并产生利润的 Agent。这一转变需要新的基础设施，例如“氛围部署”（Stripe Projects）来消除集成服务的摩擦，以及通过稳定币进行的“流式支付”来处理实时、机器速度的微交易。Sense 还强调了“超级个体”的兴起——仅在美国就有 500 万人年收入超过 10 万美元——这得益于 AI 处理了从编码到运营的所有工作。然而，她也警告了“Token 盗窃”这种新的欺诈形式，即不良行为者窃取 API Token 来转售服务或生成内容，这影响了 AI 公司超过六分之一的注册量。
