## Training Data — Memory and Continual Learning: Engram's Dan Biderman and Jessy Lin

*Training Data*

**The future of AI isn't one giant model, but millions of specialized models that continuously learn and internalize private context.**

Engram cofounders Dan Biderman and Jessy Lin argue that relying on RAG and context windows is insufficient for true continual learning; instead, we must bake knowledge directly into model weights via adapter fine-tuning. This approach allows models to "know" company-specific workflows implicitly, potentially reducing inference token consumption by up to 100x compared to retrieving documents at test time. They believe the industry needs to move beyond the "bitter lesson" of scaling compute for general intelligence and focus on infrastructure that enables efficient, personalized training for every team.

**AI 的未来不是单一巨型模型，而是数百万能够持续学习并将私有上下文内化的专用模型。**

Engram 联合创始人 Dan Biderman 和 Jessy Lin 认为，仅依赖 RAG 和上下文窗口无法实现真正的持续学习；相反，我们必须通过 Adapter 微调将知识直接烘焙进模型权重。这种方法使模型能够隐式地“知晓”公司特定的流程，与在测试时检索文档相比，可能将推理 Token 消耗减少高达 100 倍。他们认为，行业不应再局限于为通用智能扩展算力的“苦涩教训”，而应专注于为每个团队实现高效、个性化训练的基础设施。
