## AI & I by Every — How Every's Team Used AI to Ship Its Biggest Launch Ever

*AI & I by Every*

**The most effective AI workflow puts humans at the top and bottom of the "sandwich," using agents to handle the execution in the middle.**

The Every team deconstructed their recent "Builder Pack" launch, which generated record subscription revenue by bundling AI tool credits. They revealed that the success wasn't just in the bundle, but in how they used AI agents to execute the launch itself. One team member described going to the gym while an agent drafted, scheduled, and optimized four distinct email campaigns that ultimately drove $25,000 in revenue. The discussion highlighted a shift in work philosophy: instead of manually setting up A/B tests or editing video, builders should focus on defining the problem and reviewing the output. As one speaker noted, "You're at the top and bottom of the AI sandwich. You decide what the problem is, you frame the idea, and then you review the work." This approach allows experts to focus on high-level strategy and creativity while agents handle the "fake work" of configuration and implementation.

Every 团队剖析了他们最近的“Builder Pack”发布，该发布通过捆绑 AI 工具额度创造了创纪录的订阅收入。他们揭示成功不仅在于捆绑包本身，还在于他们如何使用 AI Agent 来执行发布本身。一位团队成员描述了他在去健身房的同时，一个 Agent 起草、安排并优化了四个不同的电子邮件活动，最终带来了 25,000 美元的收入。讨论强调了工作哲学的转变：构建者不应手动设置 A/B 测试或编辑视频，而应专注于定义问题和审查输出。正如一位发言人所说，“你处于 AI 三明治的顶部和底部。你决定问题是什么，你构思想法，然后你审查工作。”这种方法允许专家专注于高层战略和创造力，而 Agent 处理配置和实施的“虚假工作”。
