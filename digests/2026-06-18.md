## Training Data — Simulating Humans at Scale: Simile's Joon Sung Park

*Training Data*

**LLMs can simulate irrational human behavior if grounded in real behavioral data, not just text.**

如果基于真实的行为数据而不仅仅是文本，LLM 可以模拟非理性的人类行为。

Joon Sung Park, CEO of Simile, explains that while current foundation models excel at rational, objective tasks, they plateau at simulating human subjectivity. His company bridges the "say-do gap" by collecting real behavioral data and life stories from partners like Gallup to create agents that predict human actions with 85% accuracy. He distinguishes between "convergent simulations," where errors cancel out (like network effects), and "divergent simulations" (like elections), arguing that this technology could eventually solve fundamental mysteries in economics and social science.

Simile CEO Joon Sung Park 解释说，虽然当前的基础模型在理性、客观的任务上表现出色，但在模拟人类主观性方面遇到了瓶颈。他的公司通过与 Gallup 等合作伙伴收集真实的行为数据和生活故事来弥合“言行差距”，从而创建出能以 85% 准确率预测人类行为的 agent。他区分了误差会相互抵消的“收敛模拟”（如网络效应）和“发散模拟”（如选举），并认为这项技术最终可能解决经济学和社会科学中的基本谜题。
