**Knowledge work is evolving from "sculpting"—doing every task by hand—to "gardening," where you build systems (loops) that create conditions for AI to execute work autonomously.**

知识工作正在从“雕刻”——即亲手完成每一项任务——演变为“园艺”，即构建系统（循环）来创造条件，让 AI 自主执行工作。

Natalia explains that while AI agents like "Claudie" are excellent at executing standard operating procedures, they still require human oversight for taste and strategy. She advocates for buying specialized SaaS tools (like Atio for CRM) instead of "vibe coding" everything, because real software compiles thousands of logical rules that AI cannot one-shot. She demonstrates how Codex allows non-technical users to build complex workflows, such as an email triage system or a family caregiving portal, by defining logic and letting the model execute.

Natalia 解释说，虽然像“Claudie”这样的 AI Agent 非常擅长执行标准操作程序，但它们在品味和策略方面仍需要人工监督。她主张购买专业的 SaaS 工具（如用于 CRM 的 Atio），而不是对所有事情都进行“vibe coding”，因为真正的软件编译了成千上万个逻辑规则，这是 AI 无法一次性完成的。她演示了 Codex 如何让非技术用户通过定义逻辑并让模型执行，来构建复杂的工作流，例如邮件分类系统或家庭护理门户。
